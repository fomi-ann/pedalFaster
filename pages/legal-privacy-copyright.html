Privacy Policy

At Pedal Faster, we believe in privacy as much as we believe in wearing a helmet—it's non-negotiable.

We collect only the data we need to keep the website running like a well-oiled chain. No unnecessary tracking, no selling your info to shady bike thieves, and definitely no spam.

  • We use cookies to improve your browsing experience. If you prefer a cookie-free ride, you can adjust your browser settings.
  • If you sign up for our newsletter, we promise not to send you 300 emails a day—just the good stuff.
  • We don't share your personal data with third parties, except when required by law.

If you ever want to check, update, or delete your data, just reach out.
