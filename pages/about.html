About us

Welcome to Pedal Faster, the magazine for cyclists who live life on two wheels and laugh in the face of headwinds! Whether you're a seasoned road warrior, a weekend trail explorer, or someone who just figured out that a derailleur is not a medieval weapon, we've got something for you.

We believe cycling isn't just about getting from A to B—it's about the thrill of the ride, the satisfaction of a perfect gear shift, and the undeniable joy of coasting downhill after a climb that made you question all your life choices. We cover everything from cutting-edge bike tech and gear reviews to the best coffee stops on your route (because let's be honest, cycling runs on caffeine).

Our team of passionate cyclists, adventure seekers, and gear nerds work tirelessly to bring you top-tier content—so you don't have to pedal through a sea of boring articles. And if you've ever wondered whether spandex is really necessary for a good ride, don't worry. We've had that debate too.

So grab your helmet, pump up those tires, and join us as we explore the fast, fun, and occasionally ridiculous world of cycling.
